Grok-3+ — Scalable, Safe, and Energy-Optimized

An open implementation of the next-generation foundation model architecture

FP8 Precision

Achieves 60% memory savings over FP32 models with 8-bit floating point representation (1-bit sign, 4-bit exponent, 3-bit mantissa).

Minimal accuracy loss with carefully calibrated scaling factors while offering substantial efficiency gains.

Mixture-of-Experts Routing

Delivers 4× parameter-effective boost through sparse activation, where tokens are dynamically routed to specialized expert networks.

Top-K routing algorithm selects the most relevant experts for each token, significantly improving computational efficiency.

Safety Guardrails

Integrated formal verification hooks for monitoring and validating model outputs against safety criteria and ethical guidelines.

Comprehensive safeguards against harmful outputs, with continuous monitoring and improvement mechanisms.
